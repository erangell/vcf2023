BEFORE YOU BEGIN...

MAKE SURE YOUR COMPUTER MEETS THE REQUIREMENTS BELOW.

Internet requirements: A broadband (DSL or cable) internet connection is required to run the drill and exam segments on this site.

Computer requirements: Windows (XP or higher) running Java v. 1.4.2 or higher. If you do not have the latest version of Java, click here for the free download. Macintosh running Java under OSX. ("Leopard" or "Tiger")

Browsers: Mozilla 1.7.6 or higher, Internet Explorer 5x or above; Netscape 7x or above, Safari 3 or above. Sound card or module with MIDI and audio playback capability. Make sure Java and Javascript are enabled. Note: Certain web pages with interactive content may prompt you to allow pop-ups and downloads. This is normal behavior and you may allow the pop-up or download to run. on your browser.

Microphone: Either a built-in microphone or one that connects to the microphone input of your computer or sound card is required. If you do not have a microphone, you can buy one inexpensively at a local computer or electronics store. A professional microphone is not required.

If your system does not meet these requirements, and you are unable to get started, contact the instructor. If your system meets the above requirements, proceed to login below.
