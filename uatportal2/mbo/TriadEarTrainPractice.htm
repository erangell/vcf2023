First play the triad using the two Play buttons. Then click the quality button (M, m, +, o) for the triad that you heard. Click "Check" to check your answer. If you have difficulty, click the "Show Correct" button and play the triad again while looking at the music notation. Keep practicing until you can hear the distinct qualities of each triad.
